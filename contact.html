Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

What happens on a discovery call? +

A discovery call is a free 30-minute conversation where we learn about your organization, your current systems, and your goals. We'll ask about your EHR or ERP requirements, your timeline, and any specific challenges you're facing — then outline how we can help and what a typical engagement looks like.

Do you work with clients outside NJ and NY? +

Yes. While we're headquartered in the New Jersey / New York area, we serve clients nationwide in the United States and across Trinidad & Tobago. Many of our engagements involve remote or hybrid support — we adapt to what works best for your organization.

How quickly do you respond to inquiries? +

We respond to all inquiries within one business day. For urgent needs, call us directly at (848) 437-0158 during U.S. business hours and you'll reach a real member of our team — no chatbots, no call centers.
